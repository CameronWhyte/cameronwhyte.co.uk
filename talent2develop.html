Talent2Develop

About

Talent2Develop is an online platform designed for sports coaches and trainers to advertise their experience and qualifications to a network of people who may wish to book private lessons or sessions.

Trainers/coaches use the site to showcase their experience and qualifications, and users use the site to browse through the list of trainers and apply filters in order to find a trainer that suits their specific needs/wants.

This was a university project in which I was involved in during the final year of my degree.


My involvement

  • Manage project using the P3.express model
  • Managing & maintaining project documentation via cloud platforms
  • Leading the communication with client & project sponsor
  • Providing website content
  • Website testing & live demonstration
