Caddie Golf

About

Caddie is a golf blog, created and updated by myself.

The minimalistic design allows the content to take centre stage, and the black serif font against the plain white background was designed to give the blog content a newspaper article look.

The large, high definition images add the colour to the site and give the content a visual appeal.
