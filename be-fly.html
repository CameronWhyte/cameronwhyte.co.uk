About

This project involved adjusting an existing website that had been created using the Wix content management system. The client was looking to improve upon the front-end design of the website.

My role in this project was to apply my knowledge of user-friendly design and web design principles such as alignement, visual hierarchy and typography, in order to achieve a more professional & modern looking website for the client.


My involvement

  • Re-designed page header & navbar
  • Apply design principles such as alignement, consistency, colour scheme & typography
  • Managing, editing and updating content
